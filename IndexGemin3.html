User Guide

Open Heat & Risk

Open Heat is the total percentage of your Master Account equity currently at risk if all your stop losses were hit simultaneously.

  • It uses Principal Risk: (Entry - Stop) Ã— Remaining Shares.
  • If you move your stop to break-even or profit, that trade's heat becomes 0%.
  • Professionals typically cap total heat at 6%.

Charts

You can attach chart screenshots to your trades.

  • Copy: For example if you use TradingView, use "Copy Image" (Shift+S).
  • Paste: Tap the dashed "Paste Zone" in the Calculator or Manage Trade screen and select Paste.
  • View: Tap the thumbnail to see it full screen.

Risk Models

Equity % Model

Calculates how many shares to buy so that if stopped out, you lose exactly X% of your account balance (e.g., 1.0%). Best for consistent risk.

Position Size % Model

Calculates share count based on a fixed % of your portfolio size (e.g., 10% allocation). The risk % will vary depending on how wide your stop loss is.

Master Account

You can calculate trades for multiple accounts (e.g., Prop Firms + Personal) simultaneously.

In Settings, select one account as the Master. The Trade Log and Stats will only track the share count and P&L of this Master account to keep your data clean.

Statistics & Charts

Key Metrics
  • Win Rate: Percentage of closed trades that ended in profit.
  • Profit Factor: Gross Profit divided by Gross Loss. A value > 1.5 is generally considered good.
  • Expectancy: The average "R" (Risk Unit) you make per trade. Positive means your system has an edge.
Performance Charts
  • Account Growth: A line chart showing your cumulative performance over time based on Risk Units (R).
  • Recent Contributions: A bar chart of your last 5 closed trades. Green bars added to your growth; red bars subtracted.
  • Drawdown: Shows how far your account is from its "Peak" equity. Deep red valleys indicate periods of significant loss.
  • Monthly Performance: Aggregates your gains/losses by calendar month to show consistency.
  • Strategy & Time: Breakdowns of your performance by Strategy Tag and Time of Day/Day of Week.

Smart Insights (AI)

The App automatically scans your trade logs to find patterns you might miss. It activates once you have logged at least 3 closed trades.

  • Streak Detection: Warns you if you are on a losing streak (tilt danger) or winning streak (overconfidence).
  • Strategy Analysis: Identifies your highest performing setup.
  • Time Analysis: Compares your Morning vs Afternoon performance.

Managing Trades

Go to the Logs tab and tap the Pencil icon on any open trade.

  • Add Exit: Log partial sales (scaling out). The trade closes automatically when shares reach 0.
  • Edit Trade: Tap the icon in the modal header to fix entry prices, adjust stops, or update notes.

Data Safety

Your data is stored on this device. If you clear Safari/Browser cache, it will be lost. Use the Backup Data button in Settings regularly to save a copy to your Files or Cloud.
